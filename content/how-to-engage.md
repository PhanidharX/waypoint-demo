# How to Engage

## Intake

All requests start in **#pe-intake**. Post your request using the template below and the rotation lead will triage within 4 hours.

**Template:**
> I need [what] for [service/team] in [environment/account].
> Priority: [standard / urgent]
> Deadline: [date or "flexible"]

## Triage Outcomes

| Outcome | What happens | Typical SLA |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Self-service | We point you to the right tool or runbook | Same day |
| Quick win | Small task, picked up in current sprint | 1-2 days |
| Sprint work | Sized and scheduled into next sprint | 1-2 weeks |
| Project | Requires scoping, added to IDP or ongoing track | 2-6 weeks |
| Partner dependency | Requires coordination with another team | Varies |

## SLA by Request Type

| Type | Target | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Environment provisioning (golden path) | 48 hours | Automated pipeline |
| Environment provisioning (custom) | 10 business days | Architecture review required |
| Base image update | 1 week | Scan + validation cycle |
| Cluster upgrade | Per schedule | Rolling, canary-validated |
| Incident support | 1 hour response | During business hours |
| General intake | 4 hour triage | Rotation lead responds |

## Channels

- **#pe-intake** — All new requests start here
- **#pe-team** — Team discussions (engineers welcome to lurk)
- **#pe-announcements** — Maintenance windows, breaking changes
